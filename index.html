Why we built itvy

Travel planning is broken and fragmented. We're fixing it.

Inspiration Friction

"I saw this cool place on TikTok, but where exactly is it?" Translating visual inspiration to a map is tedious.

Static Planning

Traditional itineraries don't adapt. If your flight is delayed or you overspend, your spreadsheet won't recalculate.

Information Overload

Generic "Top 10" blog posts push you into tourist traps and make you miss the authentic, hidden gems.
